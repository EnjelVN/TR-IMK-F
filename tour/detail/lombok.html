Retreat Itinerary

Day 1

VIP Arrival, Welcome Toast & Briefing

Airport welcome service, premium vehicle transfer to the resort, check-in, followed by an elegant ice-breaking dinner session.

Day 2

Strategic Team Bonding & Beachside Fun

Morning light stretching, highly engaging team building activities on the white sand, followed by a relaxed pool lounge networking night.

Day 3

Exclusive Private Yacht Cruise to Gilis

Boarding your private luxury yacht. Enjoy premium snorkeling, cruising past Gili Trawangan, Meno, and Air, with gourmet lunch served onboard.

Day 4

Lombok Culture Exploration & Sukarara Visit

Discover Lombok's rich traditional roots. Visit Sasak Sade Village and see local weaving masters in Sukarara, ending with a scenic hill sunset view.

Enjoy spa amenities at your own pace. In the evening, dress sharp for the spectacular beachside Grand Gala Dinner featuring live music entertainment.

Day 6

Souvenir Hunt & Airport Drop Off

Pack your bags, grab premium local Lombok coffee / pearls souvenirs, and head to Lombok International Airport with unified group memories.
