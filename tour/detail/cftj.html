5-Day Cultural Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival & Keraton Yogyakarta Exploration

Airport/Station pickup, hotel check-in, then visiting the magnificent Sultan's Palace (Keraton) and Tamansari Water Castle in the afternoon.

Day 2

The Majestic Borobudur Temple & Village Tour

Morning drive to Borobudur Temple, exploring the grand Buddhist structure, followed by a peaceful horse-cart (Andong) ride around Candirejo village.

Day 3

Batik Making Class & Prambanan Sunset

Fun interactive session learning to paint traditional batik cloth, and heading to the breathtaking Prambanan Hindu temple compound for sunset views.

Day 4

Merapi Lava Tour & Culinary Heritage

A thrilling yet safe open-roof 4x4 jeep ride around the base of Mount Merapi, ending with a traditional lunch at an authentic heritage restaurant.

Day 5

Souvenir Hunting & Departure

Strolling around Malioboro street or Kotagede silver center to pick up local snacks (Bakpia) and crafts before airport/station transfer.
