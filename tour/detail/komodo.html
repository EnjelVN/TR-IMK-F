Sailing Itinerary

Day 1

Kelor Island & Kalong Sunset

Boarding the ship at Labuan Bajo Port, trekking up Kelor Island for panoramic views, and watching thousands of bats fly over Kalong Island during sunset.

Day 2

Padar Island, Komodo National Park & Pink Beach

Early morning summit at Padar Island for the famous 3-bay view, spotting wildlife at Komodo Island, and relaxing on the beautiful sands of Pink Beach.

Day 3

Manta Point, Taka Makassar & Return

Swim alongside majestic manta rays at Manta Point, explore the tiny sandbar of Taka Makassar, and cruise back to Labuan Bajo by afternoon.
