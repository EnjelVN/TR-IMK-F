8-Day Romantic Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival & Welcome Wine Service

VIP pick-up from Santorini Airport, check-in to your cliffside accommodation, followed by a relaxing welcome drink as you watch the evening sea views.

Day 2

Exploring Fira & Scenic Cliff Walk

A leisurely guided walk through the white pathways of Fira and Firostefani, visiting local boutiques and catching spectacular panoramic photos.

Day 3

Luxury Catamaran Sunset Cruise

Board a luxury catamaran in the afternoon. Swim near the Red Beach, soak in volcanic hot springs, and enjoy a fresh BBQ dinner on deck under the sunset.

Day 4

Honeymoon Photoshoot & Volcanic Wine Tour

Morning private photoshoot around Oia's blue domes. In the afternoon, indulge in a gourmet wine tasting tour across 3 historical estate cellars.

Day 5

Akrotiri Prehistoric City & Black Sand Beach

Discover the ancient ruins of Akrotiri (the Greek Pompeii), followed by a laid-back afternoon lounging at a private beach club on Perivolos black sand beach.

Day 6

Free Day for Private Relaxation

Enjoy a late morning floating breakfast, relax with an in-villa couple's spa treatment, or simply unwind together next to your private infinity pool.

Day 7

Traditional Village Pyrgos & Candlelit Gala Dinner

Explore the quiet, medieval village of Pyrgos at the highest point of the island. Celebrate your final night with an exclusive candlelit dinner at a cliffside restaurant.

Day 8

Farewell Santorini & Departure

Soak in the final morning views over the caldera, pick up local delicacies, and check-out for your private transfer to the airport/port.
