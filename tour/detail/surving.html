5-Day Surf Camp Itinerary

Day 1

Welcome to Camp & Theory Briefing

Airport pickup, check-in at the surf camp, board matching session, followed by an evening dry-land briefing on ocean safety and pop-up mechanics.

Day 2

First Splash: Kintamani/Kuta Beach Break

Morning surf session on soft sandy breaks to practice paddling, catching white water waves, and nailing the basic pop-up stance with coaches.

Day 3

Canggu Sunset Cruise & Green Wave Hype

Moving on to catch uninterrupted green waves. Afternoon video analysis to review riding postures, followed by sunset chilling at Batu Bolong beach.

Day 4

Uluwatu Road Trip & Drone Shooting

Epic trip to the southern cliffs. Watch pros or try out intermediate points around Padang Padang or Balangan with drone footage rolling.

Day 5

Graduation Surf & Departure Logistics

Final dawn-patrol surf session to show off your progress, certificate distribution, media file transfers, and afternoon airport shuttle.
