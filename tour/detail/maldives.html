7-Day Romantic Itinerary

Day 1

Malé Welcome & Scenic Water Transfer

Arrive at Velana International Airport. Greeted by resort hosts and transferred to your private island sanctuary. Welcome drinks and romantic cabin layout setup.

Day 2

Floating Floating Breakfast & Turquoise Lagoon Swim

Wake up to a beautifully served floating breakfast in your private villa pool. Spend the day swimming in the endless warm house reef lagoon.

Day 3

Couples Oasis Spa & Golden Sunset Cruise

Indulge in a relaxing 60-minute Balinese oil massage package. Later, step aboard a traditional Dhoni boat for a breathtaking sunset cruise with dolphins.

Day 4

Sandbar Picnic & Intimate Coral Snorkeling

Be dropped off at a completely secluded private sandbar for a gourmet picnic basket lunch, surrounded only by crystal clear turquoise waters.

Day 5

Water Sports Fun & Island Bike Exploring

Ride complimentary wooden bicycles across the island pathways. Try out stand-up paddleboarding (SUP) or transparent clear kayaking around the villas.

Day 6

Free Leisure & Grand Beachside Candlelight Dinner

Relax at your own pace. As the night falls, enjoy your premium private 3-course dinner set directly on the soft sand lit by torches and candles.

Day 7

Last Morning Dip & Farewell to Paradise

Enjoy a final breakfast overlooking the Indian Ocean, catch your scheduled speed transfer back to Malé, and take home endless lifetime memories.
