7-Day Overland Itinerary

Day 1

Yogyakarta Gathering & Heritage Walk

Meeting point in Yogyakarta, brief briefing, exploring Malioboro heritage corners, and welcome dinner with fellow travelers.

Day 2

Borobudur Temple & Transit to Malang

Catch the glorious morning architecture of Borobudur Temple, followed by a scenic cross-province overland drive to Malang city.

Day 3

The Majestic Tumpak Sewu Waterfall

Full day exploring the massive amphitheater drop of Tumpak Sewu waterfall. Hike down to the pristine canyon pool for epic view angles.

Day 4

Malang Cultural City Walk & Bromo Transit

Relaxed stroll around Kampung Warna-Warni Jodipan, local culinary hunting, then moving up towards the misty foothills of Bromo.

Day 5

Bromo Sunrise Adventure & Sea of Sand

Midnight 4x4 Jeep ride to Mt. Penanjakan for a magical sunrise, crossing the whispering sea of sand, and climbing up the active Bromo crater rim.

Day 6

Banyuwangi Transit & Ijen Midnight Midnight Hike

Drive towards the easternmost edge of Java. Rest early in Banyuwangi before starting the midnight ascent up Mount Ijen.

Day 7

Ijen Blue Fire, Turquoise Acid Lake & Drop Off

Witness the rare electric Blue Fire phenomenon, view the massive green turquoise acid crater lake at sunrise, head down, and finish at Ketapang Port / Banyuwangi Airport.
