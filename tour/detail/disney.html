Family Magic Itinerary

Day 1

Welcome to Tokyo & Disney Area Check-in

Arrive at Haneda/Narita airport, transfer to your kid-friendly hotel near Tokyo Disney Resort, settle into your spacious room, and explore the Ikspiari shopping mall.

Day 2

Full Day at Tokyo Disneyland

Step into a world of fantasy. Enjoy classic rides like Pooh's Hunny Hunt, meet Mickey Mouse, watch the daytime parade, and marvel at the spectacular night castle fireworks.

Day 3

Exploring Tokyo DisneySea Wonders

Experience the world's only nautical Disney park. Visit Mermaid Lagoon, ride Toy Story Mania, and watch the breathtaking "Believe! Sea of Dreams" harbor show in the evening.

Day 4

Souvenir Shopping & Departure

Enjoy a hearty breakfast, do some last-minute gift shopping at Bon Voyage (the massive Disney store), and head to the airport for your flight back home.
