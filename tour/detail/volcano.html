2-Day Open Trip Itinerary

Day 1

Midnight Gathering & Transfer to Basecamp

Late-night hotel pickup (approx. 01:30 AM), drive straight towards the foothills of Kintamani, briefing session with guides, and hot safety coffee.

Day 2

Sunrise Conquest, Hot Springs & Coffee Plantation

Trek to the summit for a stunning sunrise view over Mount Abang & Lake Batur. Descend for a relaxing hot spring soak and a tour of a local Balinese coffee plantation before heading back.
