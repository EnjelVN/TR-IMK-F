Climbing Itinerary

Day 1

Sembalun Basecamp to Sembalun Crater Rim

Briefing at Sembalun village, trek through savanna trails, and camp overnight at Sembalun Crater Rim with a view of Segara Anak lake.

Day 2

Summit Attack (3,726m) & Descent to Lake

Wake up at 2:00 AM for the summit climb to see the epic sunrise over Lombok and Bali. Trek down to Segara Anak lake and enjoy the natural hot springs.

Day 3

Lake to Senaru Crater Rim

Trek back up from the lake towards Senaru Crater Rim, capture photos of the active Baru Jari volcano, and rest for the final camping night.

Day 4

Descent via Senaru Tropical Forest & Transfer Out

Walk down through the peaceful and shady Senaru rainforest, arrive at Senaru Village, and take your private transport transfer back to the airport.
