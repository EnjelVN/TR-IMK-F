8-Day Expedition Itinerary

Day 1

Sorong Arrival & Marine Transit to Waisai

Pickup at Sorong Airport (SOQ), board the VIP ferry or private speedboat to Waisai, settle into your accommodation, and prepare dive logs.

Day 2

Check-dive & Dampier Strait Marine Life

Orientation dive at Friwen Wall. Followed by an incredible drift dive at Cape Kri to witness one of the highest fish biodiversity counts on Earth.

Day 3

Manta Sandy Station & Arborek Village

Dive with majestic reef manta rays at Manta Sandy cleaning station. Spend the afternoon taking a cultural walk around the beautiful Arborek Tourism Village.

Day 4

The Iconic Piaynemo Karst & Star Lagoon

Hike up the wooden stairs to view the legendary emerald karst island panorama of Piaynemo. Swim around the crystal-clear hidden Telaga Bintang.

Day 5

Deep Adventure to Wayag Island Cluster

An early long-range cruise to Wayag. Climb the challenging peak for the ultimate post-card view of Raja Ampat, and swim with friendly blacktip reef sharks at the beach.

Day 6

Blue Magic & Sardine Reef Deep Dive

Advanced pinnacle diving at Blue Magic and Sardine Reef to spot schooling barracudas, jacks, grey reef sharks, and the unique Wobbegong shark.

Day 7

Pasir Timbul Sandbar & Return to Sorong

Visit the magical floating sandbar of Pasir Timbul for photos, catch the afternoon ferry back to Sorong city, and celebrate with a farewell dinner.

Day 8

Souvenir Shopping & Airport Departure

Shop for local Papuan handwoven bags (Noken) or traditional snacks, and transfer back to Sorong Airport for your flight home. End of paradise tour.
