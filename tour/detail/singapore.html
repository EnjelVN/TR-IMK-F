Travel Itinerary

Day 1

Welcome to Singapore & Jewel Exploration

Arrive at Changi, explore the HSBC Rain Vortex at Jewel, check-in to your hotel, and enjoy a night walk at Clarke Quay.

Day 2

Universal Studios Singapore (USS) Full Day

Spend an exciting all-day adventure exploring the movie-themed rides and shows at Sentosa Island.

Day 3

City Landmarks & Gardens by the Bay

Visit Merlion Park, snap family photos, then head over to the spectacular Flower Dome and Supertree Grove light show.

Day 4

Science Centre & Orchard Road Shopping

Interactive science fun for children in the morning, followed by retail shopping and local culinary treats at Orchard Road.

Day 5

Souvenir Hunting & Departure

Explore Bugis Street or Chinatown for unique souvenirs before transferring back to Changi Airport for departure.
