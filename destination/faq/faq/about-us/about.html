Our Story

Born from a Single Compass and an Endless Horizon

Started by a small group of travel fanatics and oceanographers in 2013, Artemis was founded on a simple realization: the most transformative experiences lie off the beaten path, out in the elements, between destinations.

We began by organizing boutique expeditions to the Andes, focusing on immersion rather than just observation. Today, we lead over 200 annual journeys across seven continents, maintaining our commitment to small group intimacy and deep local connection.
